About - A brief introduction about me

Hello, I'm divine-architect. I'm an 18 year old self-taught programmer who excels in Computer Vision and OCR, I also dabble somewhat in NLP.
I like to play a lot of Age of Empires 2. I've been playing it since 2010.

I write blogs and quite obviously am a programmer. This site also serves as my resume. Besides age of empires, I also play War Thunder and Minecraft

I like working with embedded systems, CV and NLP. Most of my projects will be posted here.

Projects - A list of projects I've made

  • CNN based brain tumor diagnosis - A brain tumor diagnostic flask app which uses CNN to recognise tumors in MRI scans of brains
  • Image reader - A simple tool to read text from screenshots, uses OCR and the tesseract engine
  • FFS (Facial File Security) - A tool to encrypt/decrypt files using your face and a passkey, built with usability in mind
